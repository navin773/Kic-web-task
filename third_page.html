        Palakkad Fort is an old fort situated in the heart of Palakkad town of Kerala state, southern India.
        It was recaptured and rebuilt grandly by Sultan Hyder Ali in 1766 ACE and remains one of the best
        preserved forts in Kerala.The Palakkad Special Sub Jail is also located within the fort. There is a 
        small shrine dedicated to lord Hanuman known as Anjaneya Swamy temple Government has built a seperate
        garden and childrens park attached to the fort .It is a good place for spending  time with your family 
        or for couples .
